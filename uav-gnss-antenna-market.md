<p><strong>The UAV GNSS Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Helix Antennas</li><li>Patch Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UAV GNSS antenna market encompasses various types of antennas designed for Global Navigation Satellite System applications in unmanned aerial vehicles. Helix antennas are known for their circular polarization and high efficiency, making them suitable for environments with multipath signal interference. Patch antennas, on the other hand, are compact and lightweight, ideal for space-constrained UAV designs while providing good performance. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring accurate positioning and navigation for UAV operations across diverse applications.</p></p>
