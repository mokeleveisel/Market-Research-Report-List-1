<p><p>The adhesives for consumer electronics market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives, typically used for bonding intricate components, offer excellent flowability and penetration. Solid adhesives, often in the form of films or sticks, provide strong bonds with minimal mess, ideal for assembly line applications. Paste adhesives, with a thick consistency, are suitable for manual applications and help in filling gaps between surfaces. Each type serves unique functions based on application requirements and materials involved.</p></p>
