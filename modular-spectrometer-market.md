<p><p>The modular spectrometer market is categorized into three main types: IR (Infrared), UV (Ultraviolet), and Visible modular spectrometers. IR modular spectrometers are designed for analyzing materials based on their infrared absorption characteristics, often used in chemical and environmental applications. UV modular spectrometers focus on the ultraviolet spectrum, ideal for monitoring biological samples and chemicals. Visible modular spectrometers operate within the visible light range, commonly utilized in various industrial and academic settings for quality control and research purposes. Together, these spectrometers provide versatile solutions for diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
