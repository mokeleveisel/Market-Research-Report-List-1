<p><p>Pressure transmitters for the marine market are essential for monitoring various pressures in maritime applications. The gauge (relative) pressure type measures pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, making it suitable for most surface operations. Absolute pressure type measures pressure relative to a complete vacuum, providing accurate readings in deep-sea conditions. Sealed gauge pressure type features a sealed reference point, ensuring stable readings in fluctuating environments. Each type serves distinct purposes based on operational requirements and environmental conditions in marine settings.</p></p>
