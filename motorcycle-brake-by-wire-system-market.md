<p><p>The motorcycle brake-by-wire system market is categorized into two main types: single-channel and multiple-channel systems. Single-channel types utilize one electronic control circuit to manage braking for a single wheel, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution. In contrast, multiple-channel types feature distinct circuits for each wheel, enhancing stability and control during braking by allowing individual wheel modulation. This differentiation caters to varying performance needs, with multiple-channel systems typically found in high-performance and advanced motorbikes requiring greater responsiveness and safety.</p></p>
