<p><strong>The Automotive Electronic Igniter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-controlled Ignition System</li><li>Breakless Ignition System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electronic igniter market is primarily categorized into two types: contact-controlled ignition systems and breakless ignition systems. </p><p>Contact-controlled ignition systems use physical points to open and close the circuit, generating a spark for ignition. They are traditional but require maintenance. </p><p>Breakless ignition systems, on the other hand, utilize electronic components to eliminate mechanical wear and improve performance and reliability. They offer better efficiency and reduced emissions, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles.</p></p>
