<p><p>The U-Nut market is divided into two main types: Metric U-Nuts and Imperial (Unified) U-Nuts. Metric U-Nuts are designed to fit metric screws and features, commonly used in regions that utilize the metric system. In contrast, Imperial U-Nuts are tailored for use with imperial-size screws, prevalent in the United States and other countries that favor inch-based measurements. Each type serves specific industrial applications, ensuring compatibility with various fastening systems and enhancing assembly efficiency across different markets.</p></p>
