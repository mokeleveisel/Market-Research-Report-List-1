<p><p>The flare monitoring market consists of two primary types: optical flare and X-ray flares. Optical flare monitoring focuses on observing and analyzing solar flares through visible light, providing insights into solar activity and its impact on Earth. In contrast, X-ray flare monitoring detects high-energy emissions from solar flares, offering critical data for understanding the flare's intensity and potential effects on satellites and communication systems. Both types are essential for enhancing space weather forecasting and protecting technological infrastructure.</p></p>
