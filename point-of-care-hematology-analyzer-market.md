<p><p>Point of Care Hematology Analyzers are essential in providing rapid blood test results at or near the site of patient care. They are categorized into two main types: 3-Part Differentiation and 5-Part Differentiation analyzers. </p><p>3-Part Differentiation analyzers typically measure three parameters: red blood cells, hemoglobin, and hematocrit, making them suitable for general screenings. In contrast, 5-Part Differentiation analyzers provide a more detailed analysis, measuring five distinct blood cell types, which supports comprehensive diagnosis and better patient management.</p></p>
