<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence quantitative PCR instruments can be categorized into three main types based on their detection capabilities. Single-channel instruments measure one fluorescent signal at a time, making them suitable for basic applications. Dual-channel systems can concurrently detect two signals, allowing for multiplexing, which enhances experiments involving multiple targets. Multi-channel instruments offer the capability to measure several fluorescent signals simultaneously, significantly increasing throughput and complexity in assays, making them ideal for high-density studies and advanced research applications.</p></p>
