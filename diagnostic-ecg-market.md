<p><strong>The Diagnostic ECG Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting & Stress ECG Systems</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diagnostic ECG market comprises several key types, including resting ECG systems, stress ECG systems, and Holter monitors. Resting ECG systems capture the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest, providing baseline cardiac data. Stress ECG systems monitor heart function during physical exertion, helping to identify exercise-induced abnormalities. Holter monitors are portable devices used for continuous ECG recording over 24-48 hours, allowing for the detection of irregularities that may not emerge during short-term testing.</p></p>
