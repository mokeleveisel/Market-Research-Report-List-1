<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Van Wheel Speed Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed readings crucial for anti-lock braking systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors generate an electric signal based on the rotational speed of the wheel, offering reliable performance in various conditions. Both types are essential for vehicle safety and efficiency, catering to different automotive applications and requirements.</p></p>
