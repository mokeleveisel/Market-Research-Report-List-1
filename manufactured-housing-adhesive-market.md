<p><p>The manufactured housing adhesive market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Acrylic adhesives are known for their fast curing and durability, making them ideal for exterior bonding. Polyurethane adhesives offer excellent flexibility and moisture resistance, suitable for structural components. Polyvinyl acetate is commonly used for interior bonding due to its ease of use and non-toxicity. Epoxy adhesives provide exceptional strength and chemical resistance, ideal for demanding applications. Other adhesives may include silicone or hot melts, catering to niche requirements in manufactured housing.</p></p>
