Truck TRUs are typically mounted directly on large vehicles for long-haul shipments, while van TRUs are smaller and designed for urban deliveries, ensuring efficient refrigeration in compact spaces. Trailer TRUs, often used in commercial logistics, provide substantial refrigeration capacity for transporting bulk quantities over longer distances. Each type serves specific logistical needs in cold chain management.</p></p>
