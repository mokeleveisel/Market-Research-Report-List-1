<p><p>Therapeutic Drug Monitoring (TDM) involves various testing types to optimize drug therapy. Blood tests are the most common, measuring drug levels directly to ensure efficacy and minimize toxicity. Urine tests assess drug excretion, providing insights into metabolism and adherence. Saliva tests offer a non-invasive option, making it easier for patients and enabling monitoring of free drug concentrations. Other tests may include hair or tissue assays, broadening the scope of TDM for different therapeutic contexts and patient needs.</p></p>
