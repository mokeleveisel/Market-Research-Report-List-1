<p><strong>The Tannin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrolysable Tannin</li><li>Non-Hydrolysable Tannin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tannin market is categorized into two main types: hydrolysable tannins and non-hydrolysable tannins. Hydrolysable tannins, derived primarily from plant sources like oak and chestnut, can be broken down by hydrolysis, releasing phenolic compounds. They are widely used in the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries. Non-hydrolysable tannins, commonly found in sources like quebracho and mimosa, are more stable and cannot dissolve in water. They are chiefly utilized in various industrial applications, particularly in leather production and textile processing.</p></p>
