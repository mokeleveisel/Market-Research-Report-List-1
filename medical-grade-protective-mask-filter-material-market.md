<p><p>The medical grade protective mask filter material market includes primarily two types: PP Nonwoven and PTFE Nonwoven. PP Nonwoven, made from polypropylene, offers excellent filtration, breathability, and comfort, making it widely used in surgical and N95 masks. PTFE Nonwoven, or polytetrafluoroethylene, provides superior moisture resistance and filtering efficiency, ideal for high-performance applications. Both materials meet stringent regulatory standards, ensuring safety and effectiveness in healthcare settings while addressing the rising demand for reliable protective gear amidst public health concerns.</p></p>
