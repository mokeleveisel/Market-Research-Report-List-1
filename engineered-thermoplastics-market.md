<p><p>Engineered thermoplastics encompass various high-performance materials, notably PBT, PET, and PCT compounds. PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate) compounds offer excellent chemical resistance and dimensional stability, making them ideal for electrical and automotive applications. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) compounds are known for their strength and durability, commonly used in consumer goods and packaging. PCT (Polycyclohexylene Dimethylene Terephthalate) compounds provide superior thermal resistance and are suitable for more demanding environments, including electronics and industrial components, driving growth in specialized applications.</p></p>
