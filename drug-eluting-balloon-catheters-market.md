<p><p>Drug Eluting Balloon Catheters (DEB) are advanced medical devices used primarily in the treatment of vascular diseases. These catheters are designed to deliver therapeutic agents directly to the site of a vascular lesion via a balloon that releases drugs while being inflated. This targeted drug delivery minimizes the risk of restenosis and promotes better healing in the treated arteries, making DEB a valuable option for managing conditions like peripheral artery disease and coronary artery disease.</p><p>The Drug Eluting Balloon Catheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period.
