<p><p>The degassing valve market is categorized into filter integrated valves and non-filter integrated valves. Filter integrated valves are designed to expel gas while simultaneously removing impurities, ensuring cleaner output and enhanced efficiency. These are particularly valuable in applications where accuracy and quality of gas are critical. Non-filter integrated valves, on the other hand, focus solely on gas release without additional filtration. They are typically used in simpler applications where purity is less of a concern. Both types serve distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
