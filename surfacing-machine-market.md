<p><p>The surfacing machine market primarily comprises milling machines and grinding machines, each serving distinct purposes in material processing. Milling machines are used for removing material through rotational cutting tools, ideal for creating precise shapes and sizes in various materials. In contrast, grinding machines utilize abrasive wheels to achieve smooth finishes and tight tolerances, perfect for smoothing surfaces or refining edges. Both types of machines are essential in manufacturing, contributing to efficiency and accuracy in production processes across multiple industries.</p></p>
