<p><p>The PTFE anti-corrosion coatings market is segmented into two primary types: powder coatings and liquid coatings. Powder coatings consist of dry powdered resins that are applied electrostatically, providing a durable, uniform finish with excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasions. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are applied using conventional spray methods and offer versatility in application over complex geometries. Both types effectively protect surfaces from corrosion, enhancing the lifespan of industrial equipment and components in harsh environments.</p></p>
