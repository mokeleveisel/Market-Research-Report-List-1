<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Chip Capacitors (MLCC) are essential electronic components used for energy storage and filtering in various devices. The market is segmented into types based on dielectric properties: X7R, X5R, C0G (NP0), and Y5V. X7R and X5R offer moderate capacitance stability and temperature reliability, suitable for general applications. C0G (NP0) provides high stability and low loss, ideal for precision circuitry. Y5V, while allowing higher capacitance, has broader variation characteristics, making it suitable for less critical applications.</p></p>
