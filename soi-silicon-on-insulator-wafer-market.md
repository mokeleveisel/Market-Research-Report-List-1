<p><ul><li>Thick SOI Wafer</li><li>Thin SOI Wafer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SOI Silicon on Insulator Wafer market is categorized into thick and thin SOI wafers, each serving specific applications. Thick SOI wafers, typically over 100 micrometers, are ideal for power devices and high-performance applications due to their enhanced electrical isolation. In contrast, thin SOI wafers, often below 100 micrometers, are favored in low-power and high-density applications, such as advanced microelectronics and RF devices, due to their reduced thermal resistance and improved performance in lithography processes.</p></p>
