<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Two Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm compressors are classified into single stage, two stage, and multi-stage types based on their compression processes. Single stage compressors compress gas in one stroke, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. Two stage compressors use two compression stages to achieve higher pressures and improved efficiency. Multi-stage compressors involve several stages, allowing for maximum pressure compression and flexibility in handling various gases. Each type serves specific industrial needs, catering to different pressure requirements and application scenarios.</p></p>
