<p><p>Dairy fermentation starters are crucial for yogurt, cheese, and other fermented dairy products. The mesophilic type operates best at moderate temperatures (20-30°C), ideal for soft cheeses and yogurt, promoting a smooth texture and flavor. In contrast, the thermophilic type functions at higher temperatures (40-45°C), suitable for goods like yogurt and certain hard cheeses, enhancing firmness and taste. Additionally, the probiotics market focuses on starter cultures that provide health benefits, supporting digestion and immune function, thus catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
