<p><p>The metal magnetic powder core market consists of several types, including MPP Core, Sendust Core, High Flux, and Fe-Si. MPP Cores offer low core losses and high saturation, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Sendust Cores, composed of iron, silicon, and aluminum, provide excellent magnetic performance for high-precision devices. High Flux materials enhance permeability and efficiency in power applications, while Fe-Si cores are widely used for transformers due to their cost-effectiveness and decent magnetic properties. Each type serves specific electrical and magnetic needs.</p></p>
