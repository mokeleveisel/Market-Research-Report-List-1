<p><p>The electrosurgical scalpel market is divided into two main types: disposable and reusable electrosurgical scalpels. Disposable electrosurgical scalpels are designed for single-use, promoting infection control and eliminating the need for sterilization, which enhances operational efficiency. In contrast, reusable electrosurgical scalpels are intended for multiple uses, requiring proper sterilization after each surgery, which can be more cost-effective in the long run. Both types play crucial roles in various surgical procedures, offering precision and minimizing blood loss during operations.</p></p>
