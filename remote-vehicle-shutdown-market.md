<p><p>The Remote Vehicle Shutdown (RVS) market is divided into two main types: manual systems and automatic systems. Manual systems require an operator to initiate a shutdown command remotely, providing immediate control in emergency situations. In contrast, automatic systems utilize pre-programmed criteria or signals to shut down the vehicle automatically, enhancing safety and reducing the need for human intervention. Both types cater to different needs, with manual systems offering direct control and automatic systems promoting seamless integration into existing vehicle technology for enhanced security.</p></p>
